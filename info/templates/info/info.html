the band


Formed in Bristol, Cowboy Tanaka came together through friendships forged at the British and Irish Modern Music Institute (BIMM). Each member brought different influences and experiences, converging in a shared love for complex, forward-thinking music. Bristol's vibrant underground scene provided fertile ground for their experimental style, offering stages where their sharp, high-energy performances could flourish. The city's mixture of DIY spirit and artistic openness encouraged the band to push boundaries and craft something uniquely their own.

the members



Sasha


Sasha, Cowboy Tanaka's singer and rhythm guitarist, hails from Devon. Known for her sharp, angular playing style and raw yet melodic vocals, she anchors the band's complex rhythms with emotional grit. Her background in folk and punk informs her approach, blending intensity with vulnerability to shape Cowboy Tanaka's distinctive voice.


Ivan


Ivan, Cowboy Tanaka's bass player and backing singer, comes from Lviv, Ukraine. His intricate bass lines add weight and movement to the band's shifting rhythms, while his harmonies deepen their layered sound. Influenced by jazz and Ukrainian rock, Ivan brings both technical precision and soulful expression to the group's foundation.


Johnny


Johnny, hailing from Liverpool, is the lead guitarist of Cowboy Tanaka, driving the band's sound with intricate riffs and fluid, melodic lines. His style fuses math rock complexity with post-hardcore edge, giving each track urgency and depth. Inspired by Liverpool's musical heritage, Johnny adds both experimental sharpness and relentless energy to their performances.


Diego


Diego, Cowboy Tanaka's drummer from Zaragoza, Spain, is the engine behind the band's shifting rhythms. Known for his precision and explosive energy, he navigates odd time signatures with ease while keeping the music grounded. Drawing influence from progressive rock and flamenco rhythms, Diego brings dynamic power and inventive texture to their sound.

the music


Cowboy Tanaka is a four-piece math rock band known for their restless, intricate sound. Their music balances calculated precision with raw intensity, blending angular guitar riffs, shifting time signatures, and unpredictable dynamics. Two guitars often interlock in polyrhythmic patterns, while the bass anchors with jagged grooves, and the drums push the boundaries of rhythm. The result is music that feels equal parts cerebral and visceral, demanding attention yet pulling listeners into hypnotic momentum. Across three albums and one EP, Cowboy Tanaka has refined their craft, and their current tour showcases both their experimental roots and evolving, high-energy sound.
